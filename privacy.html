Privacy Policy

Recur — Subscription Tracker for iOS

Effective date: March 31, 2026

Data Collection

Recur does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All subscription data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Gmail Access

If you choose to connect your Gmail account, Recur uses read-only access (gmail.readonly scope) to search for subscription receipt emails. This access is:

  • Entirely optional — the app works fully without it
  • Read-only — Recur never modifies, deletes, or sends emails
  • On-device only — email content is processed locally and never sent to any server
  • Revocable — you can disconnect Gmail at any time in Settings

Data Storage

All data is stored on your device and protected by iOS Data Protection (NSFileProtectionComplete). Your database is encrypted at rest and inaccessible when your device is locked.

Analytics & Tracking

Recur does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. There are no third-party dependencies in the app.

Third-Party Services

Recur does not share data with any third parties. The only external service used is the Gmail API, which is user-initiated and read-only.

Children's Privacy

Recur does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.
